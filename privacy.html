Privacy Policy for MacroLens

Last Updated: January 2026

MacroLens ("we") does not store your personal data. Here is how we handle your information:

  • Food Data: Your food logs are stored directly in your own personal Google Sheet. We do not have access to this data after it is written.
  • Photos: Photos uploaded for analysis are processed by OpenAI's API and are subject to OpenAI's data retention policies. We do not save your photos on our servers.
  • Authentication: We use Google OAuth solely to write data to your spreadsheet. We do not access your contacts, emails, or other Drive files.
